Save Our Wildlife A campaign by the Global Conservation Foundation

Protecting the world's endangered species

Every year, dozens of species face extinction due to habitat loss, climate change, and poaching. This short documentary follows conservationists across three continents as they work tirelessly to protect elephants, snow leopards, and migratory birds — and what each of us can do to help.
